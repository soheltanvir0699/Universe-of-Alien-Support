How to play

Basic controls

Use Walk / Run / Stop on the bottom left and the LOOK pad on the bottom right to move the camera. Pinch to zoom. Hunt animals with the Hunt button when aimed.

Missions

Walk to red mission pins on the planet, tap Accept Mission, then solve the puzzle. Rewards are coins and gems. You can also open Missions from the left rail to see the map (view-only).

Shelter & Shop

Build Shelter with 3 Wood + 2 Stone + 1 Hide. Buy missing materials in Shop. Planets / Shelter buttons are on the right side of the HUD.

Travel

Tap Planets to open Travel Planets and move between unlocked worlds.

Coins & gems

Coins — common currency for shop goods (wood, stone, hide, etc.). Shown with a yellow coin icon.

Gems — rare currency. Earned slowly by exploring (few crystals per planet), rare hunt milestones (every 55 hunts → 1 gem), and puzzle rewards (1 gem). Used for mission lives, “see answer,” and buying coins.

Buy coins with gems (not an IAP)

In Shop → Buy coins with gems. Rate: 10 gems → 500 coins (50 coins per gem). Also 25→1250 and 50→2500. This spends gems you already own; it is not a separate App Store product.

Missions & lives

  • You start with 3 mission lives.
  • Wrong puzzle answers cost 1 life.
  • Lives refill passively: 1 life every 30 minutes.
  • Buy a life in Shop for gems, or use gems to reveal the puzzle answer.

Privacy (short)

Universe of Alien stores game progress on your device. In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not sell your personal data. Purchase receipts are handled by Apple StoreKit. Contact us via GitHub Issues for privacy questions.

App Store Review (for Apple)

To test gem packs: launch app → tap Shop (cart) → section Buy gems → purchase with Sandbox Apple ID. Gems credit immediately after a successful transaction.
