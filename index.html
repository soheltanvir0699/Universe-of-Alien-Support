FAQ

How do I play?

Use Walk / Run / Stop and the LOOK pad. Pinch to zoom. Walk to red pins, tap Accept Mission, solve the puzzle. Hunt for hide. Build Shelter from the right-side Shelter button.

What are coins and gems?

Coins buy shop goods. Gems are scarce (few planet crystals, 1 per puzzle, 1 every 55 hunts) and power lives, answer reveals, and coin exchange. Buy gem packs in Shop for real money.

Mission lives?

You start with 3 lives. Wrong answers cost 1 life. Refill 1 life every 30 minutes, or buy a life with gems in Shop.
