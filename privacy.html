UNIVERSE OF ALIEN

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Universe of Alien (“the App”), published by Sohel Tanvir, handles information when you use the iOS application.

1. Summary

The App is designed to keep gameplay progress on your device. We do not operate our own advertising network, and we do not sell your personal information.

2. Information we store on your device

  • Game progress (coins, gems, inventory, planet unlocks, missions, settings)
  • Optional Keychain / iCloud Keychain backup of save data so progress can restore after reinstall when signed into the same Apple ID

This data stays under your Apple account / device control and is not uploaded to our servers.

3. Purchases

In-app purchases (for example gem packs) are processed by Apple through StoreKit / the App Store. We do not receive your full payment card details. Apple’s privacy terms also apply to those transactions.

4. Analytics, tracking & advertising

The App does not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking for this build. We do not sell or share personal data with data brokers.

5. Children

The App is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Parents who believe a child provided information can contact us (below) and we will help remove on-device guidance where possible.

6. Data retention & deletion

Progress remains until you delete the App or clear app data. Uninstalling removes local UserDefaults data; Keychain items may persist or sync via Apple iCloud Keychain depending on your device settings. To clear progress, delete the App and (if needed) remove related Keychain entries from your Apple ID devices.

7. Your choices

  • Use the App without creating an account with us (no developer login required)
  • Manage purchases and subscriptions through your Apple ID / App Store settings
  • Control iCloud Keychain in iOS Settings if you prefer not to sync Keychain items

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.
